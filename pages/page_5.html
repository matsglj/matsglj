United World Cuisine


United World Cuisine is a 70-page cookbook with recipes from around the world.

United World Cusine was my first design project in InDesign, and where I was designing primarily for print. Additionally, I worked to market and sell the book internationally.

  • - ISBN: 978-0957374201

  • - Available: Amazon



  • 2011 - 2012


Project Development

This non-profit project was realized to raise money for the UWC Movement.

Content Organization and Preparation

After collecting recipes from more than 80 countries, I created a custom print template for the content. Each title page had color imagery, while recipe pages were simple and economically designed.

Design and Print

I designed the book to be easily navigable by regional origin. Additionally foods and spices were color coded throughout the book.

Marketing and Sales

One of the main challenges for the product was marketing and distributing the product worldwide.

Social Media Engagement

We relied on using social media like facebook and alumni newsletters. This allowed us to easily see aqcuire data on viewers geographical locations and target them. Promotions were run particularly during holidays.

Amazon as a Sales Platform

By selling the product via Amazon UK, we were able to ship our product internationally and simplify the payment process for potential buyers.
