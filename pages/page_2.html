EPIC


Rebranding Northwestern's Entrepreneurship Organization.

Leading a team of 8 students, I rebranded Northwestern's Undergraduate Entrepreurship Organization EPIC and lead successful marketing campaigns to increase its presence on campus.

  • June 2013 - May 2014

Rebranding the Organization

Rebranding EPIC was to mark the organizations new commitment to the growing entrepreneurial community on campus and in Chicago.

Design Facelift

I created a guideline to be used across teams for consistent branding in the organization, including colors, logos and custom fonts. I wanted to create a brand that appeared to be innovative and modern, but also subtle and lasting.

New Websites

I designed 4 new websites for the organizations groups with modern flat 1-page layouts. The main navigation bar easily allows users to navigate between pages in a radical, but inutitive way.

Engaging the Users

We sought to actively engage students in ongoing events and drive awareness of on-campus entrepreneurship.

Establishing an Active Online Presence

I refreshed the organizations online social media presence, branding the pages consistently across media. Setting weekly goals across each platform, we engaged users through posts, competitions, videos and a weekly newsletter.
